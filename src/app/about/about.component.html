ABOUT




We are a platform dedicated to bringing together the galleries of the world's best museums in one place. This project was developed as part of the Web Programming course for 2023/2024, with the mission to make museum visits more interactive and engaging.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide users with an innovative and fun way to explore museums. We believe that art and culture should be accessible to everyone, and that gamification can make learning and discovery even more rewarding.

What Makes Us Unique?

Gamification: On our site, you don't just visit museums; you also earn badges and achievements as you explore. Each time you visit a museum, it is recorded in your profile. The more museums you visit, the more rewards you earn!

Personalized Profile: In your profile, you can see all the museums you've visited recently and track your progress. Our achievements system encourages you to keep exploring and discovering new exhibitions.
